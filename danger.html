If you're sick, have a fever or a confirmed case of COVID-19, don't visit or accompany a patient.

Call your health care facility immediately if the ill person worsens or experiences difficulty breathing.

If you are ill with fever and cough clean hands frequently with soap and water or with alcohol-based hand rub.

Stay at home; do not attend work, school pr public places. Rest, drink plenty of fluids, and eat nutritious food.

Monitor everyone's health for symptoms such as fever, cough and if difficult breathing appears, call your health care facility immediately.

Avoid unnecessary exposure to the ill person and avoid sharing items, such as eating utensils, dishes, drink s, and towels.

Stay in a separate room from other family members, but if not possible wear a medical mask and keep a distance of at least 1 meter (3feet) from other people. Keep the room well-ventilated and if possible use a dedicated bathroom.

Special care for old people because they have a low immune system and the chance of death cases in high.
